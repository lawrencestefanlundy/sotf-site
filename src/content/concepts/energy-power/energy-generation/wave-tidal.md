# Wave / Tidal

*Kind: technology*

## Physics / mechanism

Ocean wave energy converts surface orbital motion (gravity-driven capillary/gravity waves) into electricity via oscillating water columns (OWC), point absorbers, attenuators, or overtopping devices. Tidal energy exploits predictable tidal flows through horizontal-axis turbines (analogous to wind turbines submerged in ~2–5 m/s currents) or tidal barrages. Key parameters: wave resource density ~20–70 kW/m of wavefront (Atlantic coast); tidal: 3 MW nameplate units (Orbital Marine O2, AtlantisTec AR2000) achieving ~40–45% capacity factor in strong sites. Global technically extractable resource ~2 TW (wave) and ~800 GW (tidal stream). Both remain pre-commercial at scale; LCOE sits ~£150–300/MWh versus offshore wind £50–80/MWh.

## Competitive landscape

Wave and tidal compete primarily with offshore wind for marine spatial allocation and grid connection budget. Tidal's dispatchability (fully predictable 18.6-year lunar cycle) is a genuine differentiator unavailable to wind or solar.

## Cloudberry relevance

| Technology | Capacity Factor | Predictability | LCOE (£/MWh) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Tidal stream | 40–50% | Deterministic | 150–250 |
| Offshore wind | 45–55% | Probabilistic | 50–80 |
| Wave energy | 25–40% | Statistical | 200–350 |

Adjacent: ocean thermal energy conversion (OTEC), salinity gradient (osmotic). Key component constraints: underwater drivetrain sealing, power electronics survivability, corrosion-resistant composite structures.


Cloudberry relevance is indirect but real. Marine energy converters are forcing innovation in wide-bandgap power electronics (SiC inverters for harsh, isolated grid-edge deployments), corrosion-resistant advanced materials, and subsea photonic sensing for structural health monitoring — all legitimate deep-tech deal-flow angles. Tidal's predictability makes it a credible anchor load for remote electrolysis / green hydrogen microgrids, a thesis intersection with Lunar Ventures' energy transition portfolio. Watch drivetrain and power conversion component suppliers rather than prime developers.
