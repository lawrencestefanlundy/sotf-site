# NV-Diamond Sensing

*Kind: technology · Attio deal count: 0*

## Physics / mechanism

Nitrogen-vacancy (NV) defect centers in diamond enable room-temperature quantum sensing of magnetic field, electric field, temperature, and strain via spin-dependent photoluminescence (optically detected magnetic resonance, ODMR). Spin coherence at room temperature ~milliseconds. Applications in semiconductor failure analysis: image current flow inside chips at sub-micron resolution non-invasively, identifying shorts/opens that escape conventional methods (thermal mapping, EMMI). Especially relevant for advanced packaging, heterogeneous integration, backside power delivery — where existing techniques are inadequate. Cloudberry deal pipeline: DIASENSE (Denmark, DTU spinout), with Infineon/IMEC/Mesa partnerships. Comp: QuantumDiamonds.
