# Gravity / Thermal Storage (Energy Vault, Rondo)

*Kind: technology*

## Physics / mechanism

Gravity storage stacks dense masses (concrete blocks, composite bricks) vertically using electric motors/generators; discharge inverts the motor to regenerator as mass descends. Round-trip efficiency ~75–80%. Energy Vault's GESS targets ~35 MWh per system at ~$150–200/kWh installed. Rondo Heat Battery converts off-peak electricity to high-temperature thermal (1500°C+) via resistive elements in refractory brick, storing as sensible heat; discharge via heat exchangers to industrial processes or steam turbines. Rondo claims >90% thermal round-trip efficiency, cost trajectory toward $10/kWh thermal. Both exploit low-cost bulk materials and gravitational/thermodynamic potential rather than electrochemistry.

## Cloudberry relevance

Competing directly: lithium-ion (higher round-trip ~92%, but cost/cycle degradation and supply constraints), pumped hydro (mature, geography-limited, GW-scale), compressed air (low efficiency ~60%, cavern-dependent), liquid air (Highview Power, ~60% RTE). Adjacent: molten salt thermal (CSP-integrated, higher temp ceiling ~565°C conventional, >1000°C with next-gen), phase-change materials, hydrogen storage. Gravity storage competes on capex and longevity (30+ year asset life, no degradation) rather than energy density. Rondo's thermal angle is differentiated—it targets industrial decarbonization, not grid arbitrage.

| System | RTE | $/kWh | Key constraint |
|---|---|---|---|
| Li-ion BESS | ~92% | $250–400 | Cycle degradation, supply chain |
| Energy Vault | ~75–80% | ~$150–200 | Energy density, site footprint |
| Rondo | >90% thermal | ~$10 thermal | Industrial offtake, heat-only output |

Cloudberry's materials and photonics lens matters here at the component level—advanced refractory ceramics, high-temperature sensor systems, and power electronics for grid-forming inverters are all upstream supply opportunities. GlobalFoundries-LP context: wide-bandgap semiconductors (SiC, GaN) are critical for the high-efficiency motor drives and inverter stacks both systems depend on—direct deal-flow relevance for fab-adjacent power electronics. Rondo-style industrial thermal storage also pairs with electrification plays where Lunar Ventures portfolio companies may sit.
